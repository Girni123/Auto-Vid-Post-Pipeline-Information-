Autom Gen AI Video Pipeline

Personal TikTok Publishing Automation

A small private bot that posts my pre-rendered AI videos to TikTok — so I don't have to upload every clip by hand.

What this tool actually does

Autom Gen AI Video Pipeline is a private, non-commercial utility built for one purpose: automatically publishing my own AI-generated short-form videos to my personal TikTok account.

I create the videos elsewhere, drop them into the pipeline, and the bot handles the rest — scheduling, OAuth authentication, and direct upload via the TikTok API. No manual posting, no copy-pasting captions at midnight.

This is not a public SaaS product. Nobody else can sign up, and no data is collected from third parties. It exists purely to save me time on a repetitive publishing task.

How it works

1

Create

AI videos are rendered and prepared locally as ready-to-publish files.

2

Queue

Videos enter the pipeline with captions and a scheduled publish time.

3

Publish

The bot uploads directly to TikTok via OAuth — fully automated, no manual steps.

Why this website exists

TikTok's developer platform requires a publicly reachable domain for OAuth redirects, plus a Privacy Policy and Terms of Service before API access is granted. This site fulfills those requirements — it's the official home base for the pipeline, nothing more.
